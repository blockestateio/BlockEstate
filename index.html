Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BlockEstate

Is BlockEstate promising guaranteed returns?

No. BlockEstate does not promise profits or returns. This is a long-term development project.

Do you have real estate assets today?

Not yet. Asset acquisition will begin only after Phase 3 with proper legal structure.

Is the token backed by property today?

No. Token backing will come only after legal approvals and proper compliance is established.

Is there a team allocation?

No team allocation. The team operates from the project reserve (transparent wallet). 90% goes to the community.

Why fair launch?

To keep distribution honest, community-owned, and manipulation-free. Everyone enters at the same price.
